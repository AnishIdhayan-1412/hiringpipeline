JOB SUMMARY
We are seeking a highly analytical Data Scientist to extract actionable insights from complex datasets. You will be responsible for building predictive models, designing machine learning algorithms, and automating decision-making processes to drive business growth.
CORE RESPONSIBILITIES
Data Mining: Extract, clean, and validate large volumes of structured and unstructured data.
Modeling: Develop, test, and deploy machine learning models (Regression, Classification, NLP).
Strategy: Translate technical findings into clear, visual stories and strategic recommendations for stakeholders.
Collaboration: Work with product and engineering teams to integrate models into live environments.
Optimization: Design and execute A/B tests to measure product performance and user behavior.
TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
Programming: Expert proficiency in Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn) and SQL.
Mathematics: Strong background in statistics, probability, and linear algebra.
Tools: Experience with data visualization (Tableau/Power BI) and cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure).
Education: Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field.
SOFT SKILLS
Problem-Solving: Ability to tackle open-ended business questions with curiosity.
Communication: Excellent verbal and written skills to explain data to non-technical teams.
Adaptability: Comfortable working in a fast-paced, iterative environment.
